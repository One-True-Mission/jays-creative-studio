Accessibility

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Jay's Creative Studio wants every visitor to be able to use our website and reach us easily. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA as a practical standard for accessible design.

What We Do

  • Use semantic, structured HTML so the site works with screen readers.
  • Write descriptive alt text for meaningful images.
  • Aim for readable color contrast between text and its background.
  • Support keyboard navigation, including the menu and forms.
  • Build a responsive layout that adapts to phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Respect reduced-motion settings for animations where possible.

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is something we keep working on. As we add content and features, we test and improve, and we welcome feedback that helps us do better.

Third-Party Content

Some tools we use, such as our contact form and embedded content, are provided by third parties. We choose accessible-minded providers, but we cannot fully control their accessibility.

Let Us Know

If you run into any trouble using this site, or you need information in another format, please email us at hello@jayscreativestudio.com and we will help.
