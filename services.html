Our Services

Everything Your Celebration Needs

What We Offer

Four Ways to Make It Special

Every service is tailored to your colors, your theme, and your vision. Mix and match, or hand us the whole celebration.

Quinceañera Choreography

Custom routines built around you, from the grand waltz to the surprise dance everyone will be talking about. We teach the whole court and rehearse until it shines.

  • Surprise dance and group routines
  • Father-daughter waltz
  • Court and chambelanes rehearsals
  • Choreographed to any theme or song

LED Photo Booth & Backdrops

A glowing LED photo booth that keeps the party going. Pick your backdrop color, and we design custom digital prints to match your theme. Guests leave with photos they will actually keep.

  • Four-hour rental with digital photos
  • Choose your backdrop color
  • Custom photo design to match your theme
  • Perfect for quinces, weddings, and birthdays

Custom Event Merch

Party favors and keepsakes designed around your event style. From themed sunglasses to tumblers, we make the little details feel custom and intentional.

  • Themed sunglasses and koozies
  • Tumbler cups and party necklaces
  • Surprise boxes
  • Designs matched to your colors and theme

Handmade Kraft Banners

Hand-lettered banners on brown Kraft paper, made to whatever size your space needs. A warm, custom touch for the dessert table, photo wall, or entrance.

  • Brown Kraft paper banners
  • Any size you need
  • Custom lettering and names
  • For quinces, weddings, and any event
