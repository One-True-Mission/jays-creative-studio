Jay Founder & Designer
Meet Jay

Made by Hand, Made for You

Jay's Creative Studio started with a simple love for celebrations and a knack for making them feel special. What began with choreography and a few handmade touches grew into a full event studio serving families across Central Texas.

Jay designs every piece herself, from the photo booth backdrops to the banners and party merch, so each celebration feels personal, never pulled off a shelf. The goal is always the same: make your event feel as one-of-a-kind as the person it is for.

Quince, wedding, birthday, or school event, we would love to be part of your story.
