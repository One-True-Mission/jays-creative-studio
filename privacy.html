Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Jay's Creative Studio ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you use our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. We serve clients in Texas, and this policy is written to align with the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA).

Information We Collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit an inquiry form, we collect your name, email, and any phone number, event date, event type, and details you choose to share.
  • Booking and order details. If you book a service or order custom merch, we keep the information needed to fulfill it, such as your event details, designs, and delivery preferences.
  • Automatic information. Like most websites, basic technical data (such as browser type and pages visited) may be collected by our hosting provider for security and performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide a quote.
  • To plan, design, and deliver the services or products you book.
  • To communicate about your event, scheduling, and payment.
  • To improve our services and keep the site secure.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to run our business, including with:

  • Formspree, which processes our contact form submissions and delivers them to us.
  • Our hosting provider, which serves this website.
  • Payment or print vendors, only if you place a paid order, and only the details needed to complete it.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies, mainly for basic functionality and security. You can control cookies through your browser settings. The site works without them.

Data Retention

We keep inquiry and booking information only as long as needed to serve you and to meet ordinary business and legal requirements, then we delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your state, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and to ask us not to sell or share it (we do not sell it). To make a request, email us and we will respond as required by law.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for adults booking events. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted here with an updated date.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@jayscreativestudio.com.
